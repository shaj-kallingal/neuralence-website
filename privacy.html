Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 21, 2025

Ozole Digital is a platform for creators and retail businesses to accept support from their audience, share exclusive content, manage promotional activities, and run loyalty programs. At Ozole Digital, your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to ensuring that your personal information is secure when using our products and platform.

About Us

Our services are provided by Publisher Inc (hereinafter referred to as “Ozole Digital”), an Indian company with a registered address at 2211, 2/1149/I 100, HiLite Business Park, Iringallur, Kozhikode 673 014, Kerala, India. It is our policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our website and associated services.

Website Visitors

Like most website operators, Ozole Digital collects non-personally-identifying information typically made available by web browsers and servers, such as browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Our purpose in collecting this information is to better understand how visitors use our website. We may release non-personally-identifying information in aggregate, e.g., through reports on website usage trends.

We also collect potentially personally-identifying information, such as Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, for logged-in users and those making payments on OzoleDigital.com. IP addresses of logged-in users and payees are disclosed only under the same circumstances as personally-identifying information, as described below. Payee IP addresses and email addresses are visible to Ozole Digital administrators and handled by payment processors during payment processing.

Payments

To make a payment to a creator on Ozole Digital, you must provide payment information to our payment processor, Stripe. They provide us with a token representing your account, your card’s expiration date, card type, and the last four digits of your credit card. If you provide a name and email address, this information is also shared with us.

We collect and process information about the creators you support, the level of support, rewards received, and the frequency of support.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain interactions with Ozole Digital, such as signing up or making transactions, require us to collect personally-identifying information. For example, users signing up at OzoleDigital.com provide a username and email address. For transactions, additional personal and financial information may be required. We collect only the information necessary to fulfill the purpose of your interaction with us and do not disclose it except as described below. You may refuse to provide personally-identifying information, though this may limit your ability to engage in certain activities.

To delete your personal data, email us at support@ozole.in.

Aggregated Statistics

We may collect statistics about visitor behavior on our websites and may display or share this information publicly. However, personally-identifying information is not disclosed except as described below.

Protection of Personally-Identifying Information

Ozole Digital discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to employees, contractors, and affiliated organizations that (i) need it to process on our behalf or provide services and (ii) have agreed not to disclose it. Some of these parties may be located outside your home country, and by using our websites, you consent to the transfer of such information. We do not rent or sell this information. Disclosure may occur in response to legal requests or to protect Ozole Digital’s property, rights, or the public. Registered users may receive emails about new features, feedback requests, or updates. We take reasonable measures to protect against unauthorized access, use, alteration, or destruction of this information.

Account Deletion

You may delete your account through the Ozole Digital platform, which will remove all associated data. If you have a membership, cancel your subscription per our cancellation terms to stop recurring payments. Merely deleting your account without canceling your subscription will not stop these payments.

Cookies

Ozole Digital uses cookies to identify and track visitors, their usage, and preferences. Cookies include:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for site functionality, such as logging in and accessing secure areas. These cannot be refused.
  • Analytics/Performance Cookies: Used to understand user behavior via Google Analytics, which may store your IP address on servers in the United States. Some cookies expire at session end, while others may remain for up to two years. Opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Visitors can refuse cookies via browser settings, but this may affect website functionality. Learn more at All About Cookies.

Business Transfers

In the event of an acquisition, bankruptcy, or closure of Ozole Digital, user information may be transferred to a third party, who may continue to use it as per this policy.

Ads

We do not run external advertisements but use ad networks like Twitter Ads for retargeting. This policy covers our use of cookies, not those of advertisers.

Prohibited and Restricted Content

We aim to maintain a safe environment for creators and communities. Prohibited content includes:

  • Harassment: Content that harasses or threatens individuals or groups.
  • Hate Speech: Content promoting violence, hate, or dehumanization based on protected characteristics.
  • Personal Information: Private or confidential information, such as credit card details or medical records.
  • Impersonation: Pretending to be another person, group, or authoritative source.
  • Misinformation: Deceptive or misleading health, medical, or civic information.
  • Sexually Explicit Content: Pornography, nudity, or content promoting explicit behavior.
  • Regulated, Dangerous, & Illegal Content: Content promoting dangerous activities, illegal acts, or terrorism, or infringing on legal rights.
  • Child Safety: Content depicting the nudity of minors.

Report violations to us or block offending creators to stop seeing their content.

International Users

Ozole Digital is based in India. Personal data may be transferred to servers in India or to service providers globally. For EU users, we ensure adequate protection under GDPR. By using our services, you consent to data transfers and processing as per this policy.

Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this policy at our discretion. Check this page for changes, and if you have an account, we may notify you of updates. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of changes.

Other Terms and Conditions

Your use of Ozole Digital is subject to our Terms of Use. By agreeing to this policy, you also agree to Google’s Privacy Policy for services we use, such as Google Sign-In, reCAPTCHA, and Analytics.
